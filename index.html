Our Story

Maria and Eduardo first met at a music concert back in November of 2018. They hit it off by dancing the night away while talking to one another, noticing that they had many things in common. Dancing, concert-going and music where the main topics of that night. The more they danced and talked, the more they started falling for each other. From that day forward, the life of a college student and the life of a United States Marine commenced on a journey that saw many smiles, laughters, tears and frowns. A journey that both want to tackle for the rest of their lives together, as a team, best friends, and partners. Who knew that with a simple question the first night they met would change the rest of their lives for the better, “Bailamos?”, “Would you like to dance with me?”
